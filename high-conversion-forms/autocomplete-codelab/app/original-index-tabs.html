Autofill in action

To try this example:
  1. Open it in Chrome (version 42+) and start filling the fields.
  2. Chrome will suggest you 'auto fill' data from what it got so far.
  3. For extra points, open Chrome DevTools and checkout the attributes we used on each field.
  4. You can also try to click on the 'Checkout' button at the bottom of the form and see the validation in action.
